This is the translated diary of a velociraptor named Bidey Gonsuun during his last days on earth

Entry #1


Dear Diary:

   Today was disappointing. Even though the hunt went well, that bonelicker Thynee Peepy took all the credits. He stole my prey made the entire pack thought it was him who brought it down. Only my sweet Clawdia Dymore knew the what happened. She tried to tell the truth but no one believed her. Everyone praised him like he's the brightest star in the night sky, but my favorite spark of the black veil is not that one. It's the tiny one that's getting bigger every night. It's a lot like me. We both started out small but grew steadily. Soon I will challenge that scaley bastard. I'll show everyone that I'm the real pack leader.

Dear Diary:
